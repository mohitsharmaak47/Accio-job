The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound,similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound.The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as beagling.Possessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts, the beagle is primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. The beagle is intelligent and is popular pet due to its size, good temper, and a lack of inhertied health problems.
